\section{Affirmation of Principles}

By signing below, I affirm that:

\begin{enumerate}
  \item I have read and agree with the Principles of the Anti-Federalist Party as set forth in Article~II of the Party Constitution.
  \item I believe that the government closest to the people serves the people best.
  \item I support the decentralization of political power from federal and state governments to local communities.
  \item I oppose the fusion of corporate and governmental power.
  \item I commit to civic participation in my local community.
\end{enumerate}

\afpcallout{Membership in the Anti-Federalist Party does not require agreement on every policy issue. It requires agreement on one structural principle: power belongs in the hands of communities, not distant capitals. We welcome members across the political spectrum who share this commitment.}
